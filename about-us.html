About School

ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Management
THE STANLEY MATRICULATION HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL is managed by The STANLEY EDUCATIONAL TRUST PAPPIREDDIPATTL.
Evoluation of the School

The School is established to provide thorough knowledge English language skill right from the pre-school level. We have professionally personalized guidance supervision. The school ha fully modernized infrastructure with all necessary facilities.

Medium of Instruction

English is the designated medium of instruction for classes conducted under the Matriculation Board and the Higher Secondary Board (English medium). Both teachers and students are strictly instructed to communicate exclusively in English within the campus premises. Our school takes pride in being the only institution in this region that emphasizes the practical application of the English language within the campus. This commitment ensures a comprehensive and immersive learning experience for all our students.

VISION

"We mould students to be the good citizens Education is the birthright of every child. A child is born with immense potential Ines The STANLEY has a vision of education system/where no child is ever rejected where the child is the centre of every learning activity/where personal attention is given to every child focusing on Physical, intellectual, emotional and spiritual strengths which are four main pillars of education.

MISSION

To provide a strong foundation for the child in education ta become a leader in the shifting and changing global scenario

To establish an institution where children grow up in the natural environment and the STANLEY such ambience

To provide state-of-the art facilities to the students to develop themselves. "Learning by doing" is the motto, where learning is pleasure.

To mould a child to be society-committed and globally Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School is located in Pappireddipatty, Dharmapuri. There are 35 dedicated and professional faculty members who impart quality education to children in this State Board school.

Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School was launched in 1988. With the student teacher ratio being 40:1 the primary medium of instruction is English and Tamil as secondary medium. The school tries to provide best learning environment.

Objectives

At Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School, our objectives are clear, aiming at the holistic development of each student, nurturing their innate potentialities and instilling noble values and virtues. Here's a comprehensive overview

  • Focused on the comprehensive development of the child, considering and nurturing all inherent capabilities.

  • Instil ideal values and virtues in children, fostering noble social thinking and ways of life.

  • Promote character formation and a humanitarian outlook, emphasizing values such as love, cooperation, independence, self-reliance, and commitment to the poor and the needy.

  • Contribute to educational research, innovations, and reforms across various fields related to education.

  • Equip the child to fulfil commitments to society and contribute positively to their community.

  • Provide quality education designed for the all-round development of the child, preparing them for the challenges of the future. Emphasis on intellectual, moral, social, and professional enlightenment with special focus on virtues like truthfulness, honesty, politeness, industry, simplicity, and self-discipline.

  • Create a healthy physical and social environment that supports continuous improvements and growth, fostering a positive attitude towards education.

  • Develop students' language skills, particularly in English, to enhance communication abilities.

  • Enable students to learn and grow from the challenges in their coursework, fostering independent thinking.

  • Empower students to recognize and realize their values and talents.

  • Encourage students to develop a sense of responsibility towards themselves, others, and their community.

    Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School is dedicated to providing an enriching educational experience that goes beyond textbooks, nurturing well-rounded individuals prepared for a dynamic future.

About Us

Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School was founded by Mr. V. MURUGESAN M.A., a Trustee of Stanley Educational Trust, in the year 1979. Mr. V. MURUGESAN serves as the Correspondent of the school. The school is nurtured by the efficient and dedicated endeavours of our staff, who strive to bring out the best in each child. We reinforce every child's budding energy to help them flourish.
