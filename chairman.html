Chairman Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers,


Welcome to Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School, a nurturing space for holistic education and character development.


At Stanley, we believe in fostering an environment where students not only excel academically but also grow into responsible, compassionate individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow. Our commitment to providing quality education is driven by the vision of our esteemed Chairman, Mr. V. Murugesan, M.A.

Mr. Murugesan's dedication to education has been recognized with several prestigious titles such as Uzhaippal Uairntha Maamanithar, Kalvichemmal, Kalvikavalar, Sadhanaiyalar, Uzhilipen Sigaram, Sevaisuder, and Indian Achiever. His visionary leadership has steered our school to numerous achievements and awards, making it the Best School in the Dharmapuri District for four consecutive years.

Under his guidance, we have maintained a 100% pass rate in public exams, securing State Third Rank in the 10th standard for the academic year 2013-2014. Additionally, our school has clinched the overall state level championship in Karate for the past eight years.

Mr. Murugesan's emphasis on a well-rounded education is reflected in the school's approach, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. Our students not only succeed academically but also actively participate in co-curricular activities, sports, and community service, developing into confident and responsible citizens.

As we embark on this journey of learning and growth, we invite parents and guardians to be active partners in their child's education. Together, we can create a nurturing environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning.

Thank you for choosing Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School. We look forward to a successful and enriching academic year.

Mr. V. Murugesan, M.A

About Us

MURUGESAN serves as the Correspondent of the school. The school is nurtured by the efficient and dedicated endeavours of our staff, who strive to bring out the best in each child.
