Stanley Mat. Hr. Sec. School,

Welcome to Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School, a proud initiative managed by The Stanley Educational Trust Pappireddipatty. Since its inception in 1988, our institution has been dedicated to providing a solid foundation of education, fostering a love for learning from the pre-school level onward.
Our school is committed to equipping students with a thorough knowledge of the English language and essential skills. Under the professional and personalized guidance of our faculty, we strive to create an environment where learning is not just a task but a joyful experience. The school boasts fully modernized infrastructure with all necessary facilities, ensuring aconducive learning atmosphere.

Medium of Instruction

English is the designated medium of instruction for classes conducted under the Matriculation Board and the Higher Secondary Board (English medium). Both teachers and students are strictly instructed to communicate exclusively in English within the campus premises. Our school takes pride in being the only institution in this region that emphasizes the practical application of the English language within the campus. This commitment ensures a comprehensive and immersive learning experience for all our students

About Us

Stanley Matriculation Higher Secondary School was founded by Mr. V. MURUGESAN M.A., a Trustee of Stanley Educational Trust, in the year 1979. Mr. V. MURUGESAN serves as the Correspondent of the school. The school is nurtured by the efficient and dedicated endeavours of our staff, who strive to bring out the best in each child. We reinforce every child's budding energy to help them flourish.
